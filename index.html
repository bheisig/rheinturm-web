Rheinturm Digital Clock

This website shows you the current local time. It's based on the sculpture Lichtzeitpegel mounted on the Rheinturm which is located in Düsseldorf, Germany.

The picture was shot by Matthias Neugebauer and is licensed under CC BY-NC 2.0.

Who I am and why I built this site

I'm Benjamin Heisig and I live in the beautiful city of Düsseldorf. To share my love for the city and especially for the Rheinturm sculpture I created this little site.
